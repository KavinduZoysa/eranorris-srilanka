About Eranorris

Eranorris was created with a simple idea — to offer peaceful villas and authentic local experiences that reflect the true spirit of Southern Sri Lanka.

We are a locally hosted collection of private villas in Rathgama and Hikkaduwa, designed for travelers who value calm, comfort, and meaningful experiences.

From cinnamon harvesting traditions to kayaking in quiet lagoons, we believe Sri Lanka is best experienced slowly — not rushed.

Our Philosophy

  • ✔ No mass tourism
  • ✔ Personal hosting
  • ✔ Carefully curated stays
  • ✔ Experiences rooted in local culture

We don’t aim for one-time stays. We create experiences that make you want to return.
